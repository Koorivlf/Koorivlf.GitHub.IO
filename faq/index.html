[General Commission Process]

-- This process is subject to change. This is a general overview of the process.

  • I. I will post how to get a commission / You may inquire about commissions.
  • II. You will send information* I need to complete the commission.
  • III. After I approve of the idea, a rough sketch will be sent to the you for review.
  • IV. Once the you approve of the sketch, I will start work on the final piece.
  • V. I'll send some WIP stuff depending on the commission type
  • VI. I will send the completed artwork to you. If no changes need to be made, the artwork may be posted to my galleries.

*Character references, description of artwork, privacy of artwork, etc.

[Will you use my exact color codes on my ref?]

-- Probably not. I eyeball the colors I use the majority of the time because colors are relative to other colors around it. Using exact colors from refs may cause weird results or not allow me to light the picture correctly.

[What tools/software do you use?]
