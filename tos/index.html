[General Commission Expectations]

These expectations are subject to change depending on the type of the Commission.

  • II. The time needed for the Artist to complete the work depends on the Commission type and the current work queue. Discuss with the Artist about any deadlines that need to be met before the Commission is accepted.

[Alterations]

I. The Artist can make alternations depending on the progress and type of Commission:

  • Idea Phase

  • Sketch Phase
    • Minor changes and 2 major changes can be made as long as it pertains to the Commission type.*

    • The Client can still request for edits*

    • Any mistakes made by the Artist will be fixed within reason.

[Refunds and Cancellations]

I.The Client can request for a cancellation of a Commission. The base refund fee is 5 USD. If the Client has a valid reason that the Artist will accept, the fees will be waived or lowered.

Otherwise, the Client has the right to:

  1. Cancel the Commission and inform the Artist of the decision before the payment is due. No refund fee will be incurred.
  2. If payment is sent to the Artist but work has not started, the payment minus any fees incurred will be refunded by the Artist back to the Client.
  3. If payment is sent to the Artist and work has started, up to 60% of the payment + any fees incurred will be kept by the Artist and the rest will be refunded back to the Client.
  4. If the Artist is near completion or is completed, no refund will be given to the Client.

II. If the Commission isn't completed within 5 months of the Artist receiving payment, a full refund will be given.

III. If the Artist is unable to complete the obligations under the terms of this Agreement due to uncontrollable circumstances such as but not limited to natural disasters or severe medical injuries, a full refund with be given if possible.

IV. Mature content will not be made for minors. If a Client who is not an adult, under 18 years old (USA law), is found trying to inquire about mature content, the request will be declined. If the Client tries to hide the fact that they are a minor while inquiring about or buying mature content, they will be banned from getting any future Commissions from the Artist.
